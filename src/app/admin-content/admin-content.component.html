Administration 

      
Responsible for overseeing hospital governance, strategic planning, financial management, and policy development to ensure effective operation and compliance with regulatory standards.

    

  
    
    

      
Operations

      
Manages day-to-day hospital operations, including facility management, housekeeping, security, and maintenance, to ensure smooth functioning and a safe environment for patients and staff.

    

  
    
    

      
Clinical Services

      
Coordinates medical care provided by healthcare professionals, including physicians, nurses, and allied health professionals, to diagnose, treat, and manage patient conditions effectively and efficiently.

    

  
    
    

      
Patient Care Services

      
Focuses on delivering high-quality patient care experiences, including patient admission, care coordination, advocacy, and support services, to promote patient well-being and satisfaction.

    

  
    
    

      
Support Services

      
Provides essential support functions such as information technology, finance and accounting, human resources, quality management, and compliance to enable the effective operation of the hospital and its services.

    

  
    
    

      
Ancillary Services

      
Offers additional services such as food and nutrition, rehabilitation, medical equipment and supplies, and transportation to meet the diverse needs of patients and enhance their overall healthcare experience.

    

  
    
    

      
Community and Public Health Initiatives

      
Engages with the community to promote health education, disease prevention, and wellness initiatives, and collaborates with public health agencies to address public health challenges and improve population health outcomes.

    

  
    
    

      
Research and Development

      
Conducts clinical research, innovation, and development activities to advance medical knowledge, technology, and treatments, ultimately improving patient care and outcomes through evidence-based practices and discoveries.

    


    
  

    
Finance and Accounting

    
Manages financial planning, budgeting, billing, revenue cycle management, and financial reporting to ensure the financial health and sustainability of the hospital.

  


  
  

    
Information Technology

    
Oversees hospital information systems, electronic health records (EHR), IT infrastructure, and cybersecurity to support efficient and secure data management and technology operations.

  


  
  

    
Human Resources

    
Handles recruitment, training, staffing, employee relations, and compliance with labor laws to support the hiring, development, and retention of qualified healthcare professionals.

  


  
  

    
Quality Management and Patient Safety

    
Ensures compliance with quality standards, accreditation requirements, and patient safety initiatives through continuous quality improvement, risk management, and safety protocols.

  


  
  

    
Risk Management and Compliance

    
Identifies and mitigates risks, ensures regulatory compliance, and manages liability issues to protect the hospital, staff, and patients from legal and financial liabilities.

  


  
  

    
Rehabilitation Services

    
Provides physical therapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy, and other rehabilitation services to help patients recover from injuries, surgeries, or medical conditions and regain functional abilities.

  



  
  

    
Telemedicine Services

    
Provides virtual healthcare services, remote consultations, and telemonitoring to enhance access to care, improve patient outcomes, and facilitate remote collaboration among healthcare providers.
